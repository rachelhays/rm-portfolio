Media Sample 1

"Houses Made of Clay"

This media element was a project for a graduate course in the Instructional Design Technology (IDT) program at the University of Memphis. The objective was to create a story that both addresses an issue and educates an audience on a potential solution.


This story spotlights a type of earthen home building material called "superadobe," which may be a solution to avoid devastation due to some natural disasters.
